The Process

Your Role as a Network Marketer

Your job is simple but powerful: Help us onboard LinkedIn connects (real user accounts) from individuals around you.

1

Find Individuals

Identify individuals with active LinkedIn accounts in your network.

2

Guide Onboarding

Help them through the onboarding process with our training and support.

3

Earn Commissions

Receive commissions based on your results and the number of connects.

Detailed Steps

The Complete Process

Here's a detailed breakdown of how our network marketing system works.

01

Joining the Network

Sign up to become a network marketer with Luvsmith Corp. There's no capital required to start, and you'll receive immediate access to our training materials and support system.

02

Training & Preparation

Complete our comprehensive training program to understand how to effectively identify and onboard LinkedIn connects. You'll learn about our ethical standards and best practices.

03

Finding LinkedIn Users

Reach out to individuals in your network who have active LinkedIn accounts. Explain the opportunity and how their accounts will be ethically used for professional purposes.

04

Onboarding Process

Guide LinkedIn users through our simple onboarding process. This includes verifying their accounts and ensuring they understand how their accounts will be used.

05

Tracking & Reporting

Use our system to track the LinkedIn connects you've onboarded. Our dashboard provides real-time updates on your progress and earnings.

06

Receiving Payments

Get paid every 10 days based on the number of active LinkedIn connects you've onboarded. Payments are transparent and consistent.

Our Ethics

What We Use the Accounts For

We're committed to ethics, transparency, and safety in all our operations.

The LinkedIn accounts are used ONLY for:

Professional campaigns

Client promotions

Verified research

Expanding trusted LinkedIn networks

All account owners are informed, involved, and protected throughout the process.

Requirements

What Makes a Valid Connect

To ensure quality and authenticity, every LinkedIn account (connect) must meet these requirements:

Real Person

Be owned by a real person with a genuine identity.

Active Profile

Have a photo and complete profile information.

Accessible

Be accessible by the owner at all times.

Verified

Be verified with email or phone number.
