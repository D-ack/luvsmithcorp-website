What Makes a Valid Connect

We maintain high standards for the LinkedIn accounts we work with to ensure quality and authenticity.

Every LinkedIn account must be owned by a real person with a genuine profile.

Active Profile

The account must be active with a photo and complete profile information.

Accessible

The account must be accessible by the owner at all times.

Verified

The account must be verified with email or phone number for security.
